  worker-d1.js = Kitchen (backend, handles orders)
  mastering.js = Menu/Waiter (frontend, shows options to customers)

  - Customer orders (mastering.js sends request)
  - Kitchen receives order (worker-d1.js)
  - Kitchen prepares food (mastering server)
  - Customer gets food (mastering.js shows result)
