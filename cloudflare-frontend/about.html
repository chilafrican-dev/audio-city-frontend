About Audio City

Uganda's Premier Music Community Platform

Our Story

Audio City was developed and founded by Nico Pan Beats, a Ugandan music producer who recognized a critical gap between music fans and artists. After witnessing the challenges artists face in connecting with their audience and the difficulty fans experience in discovering new music, Nico Pan Beats created Audio City to bridge this divide.

Our mission is to create a vibrant, inclusive platform where music creators and fans can connect, discover, and share the rich musical culture of Uganda and beyond. We believe in empowering artists, fostering creativity, and building a community that celebrates music in all its forms.

Our Values

  • Creativity First: We celebrate artistic expression and support creators at every stage of their journey.
  • Community: We believe in building a supportive, respectful community where everyone can thrive.
  • Innovation: We continuously improve our platform with new features and tools to serve our community better.
  • Accessibility: We're committed to making music accessible to everyone, regardless of background or experience.
  • Safety: We maintain a safe environment through automated content moderation and community guidelines.

For Artists

Audio City provides everything you need to share your music with the world:

  • Easy track uploads with support for MP3, WAV, and FLAC formats
  • Professional mastering tools to enhance your sound
  • Custom cover art generator to create stunning visuals
  • Direct connection with your audience and fans
  • Analytics to track your music's performance
  • Monetization options through donations and support

For Music Lovers

Discover and enjoy music like never before:

  • Browse trending tracks and hot charts
  • Follow your favorite artists and get notified of new releases
  • Create playlists and share your discoveries
  • Connect with other music enthusiasts
  • Support artists directly through donations
  • Explore diverse genres from Afrobeat to Kidandali, Amapiano, and more

Safety & Moderation

We take community safety seriously. Audio City uses advanced automated systems to detect and prevent:

  • Inappropriate content and profanity
  • Hate speech and violent language
  • Spam and misleading information
  • Copyright violations

Our moderation team works around the clock to ensure a positive experience for all users. Learn more in our Community Guidelines.
