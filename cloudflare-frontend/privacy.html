Privacy Policy

How we protect and use your information

Introduction

Audio City respects and protects user privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal information when you use our platform. By using Audio City, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and profile information you provide when creating an account
  • Artist Details: Artist name, bio, profile picture, and other information you choose to share on your profile
  • Transaction Data: Payment information processed through our payment provider (Flutterwave), including transaction amounts and payment methods
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the platform, including tracks you listen to, artists you follow, and content you upload
  • Device Information: Browser type, device type, IP address, and other technical information necessary for platform functionality

How We Use Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To provide, maintain, and improve our music platform services
  • Payment Processing: To process payments, donations, and transactions securely through Flutterwave
  • Communication: To send you important updates, respond to your inquiries, and provide customer support
  • Platform Improvement: To analyze usage patterns, improve user experience, and develop new features
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes
  • Content Moderation: To detect and prevent inappropriate content, spam, and violations of our community guidelines

Payment Security

All payments on Audio City are processed securely through Flutterwave, a trusted payment processor. Audio City does not store your credit card details, mobile money information, or other sensitive payment data. All payment information is encrypted and handled directly by Flutterwave in accordance with industry-standard security practices.

When you make a payment, you are redirected to Flutterwave's secure payment gateway, and your payment details are never shared with or stored by Audio City.

Information Sharing

Audio City does not sell your personal data to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Payment Processors: We share necessary transaction information with Flutterwave to process payments securely
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information when required by law, court order, or government regulation
  • Service Providers: We may share information with trusted service providers who assist in operating our platform, subject to strict confidentiality agreements
  • Platform Safety: We may share information to prevent fraud, abuse, or violations of our terms of service

We do not share your personal information for marketing purposes with third parties without your explicit consent.

Cookies

Audio City uses cookies and similar technologies to improve functionality and analyze site usage. Cookies help us:

  • Remember your preferences and login status
  • Understand how you use the platform to improve user experience
  • Provide personalized content and recommendations
  • Analyze traffic patterns and platform performance

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences, though disabling cookies may affect some platform features.

Data Protection

Audio City implements reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication and access controls
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we are committed to maintaining industry-standard protections.

User Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: You can update or correct your personal information through your account settings or by contacting us
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal and operational requirements
  • Data Portability: You can request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Opt-Out: You can opt out of certain communications and data processing activities

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the contact information provided below.
